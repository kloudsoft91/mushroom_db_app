Welcome to Mushroom Finder

There are lots of interesting mushrooms to discover all around where we live. I've seen at least 10 different species in my back yard the last month or so. Only one of those is edible. Mushroom identification is tricky. There are often only subtle differences between something delicious and a day or two of "severe gastrointestinal distress". One to two species can kill you, but it's a week before you start to feel symptoms.
